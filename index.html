Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two teams of five players each, aiming to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. The game is characterized by dynamic movement, strategic plays, and quick transitions between offense and defense. Players use dribbling, passing, and teamwork to advance the ball, with different point values assigned to shots based on their distance from the hoop. The team with the most points at the end of the game or quarters emerges as the winner, making basketball a thrilling and globally popular sport.
